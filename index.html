  • Andhra Pradesh: First flood warning issued at Prakasam Barrage in Vijayawada

    The first flood warning has been issued at Prakasam Barrage in Vijayawada of Andhra Pradesh on October 15. The flood alert was issued after the flow in Krishna river touched 4.07 lakh cusecs The discharge was steady at 4.5 lakh cusecs at 5 p.m. The first flood warning was issued for the fourth time this year since August and several areas bore the brunt of the floods.Villages in several mandals including Avanigadda, Nagayalanka, Ghantasala and Movva in Krishna district are also affected by the flood.
